Realme 5s Details

The Realme released a new smartphone “5s″. It is an affordable budget device that can fit into any type of requirement. The smartphone runs with the Android 10.0; ColorOS 6 operating system. The device sports a 6.5″ inch IPS LCD having a screen resolution of 720 x 1600pixels, and a 20:9 aspect ratio, and a density of 269PPI. The phone comes with a 48MP, 8MP, 2MP, 2MP quiet primary camera with an LED flash and a 13MP selfie camera. You can record videos at 2160p resolution and @30fps. The Realme 5s has 4GB RAM and 128GB of inbuilt storage options. The phone is powered by a 4×2.0 GHz Kryo 260 Gold Octa-core processor with the Snapdragon 665 (11 nm) chipset. Connectivity options include 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 5.0, micro USB, WiFi Direct, dual-band, hotspot, etc. This phone comes with a non-removable Li-Poly (Lithium Polymer) 5,000mAh battery.
